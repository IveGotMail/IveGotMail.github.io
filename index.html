Theology

PythonClaude APIMulti-agentAgentic AIPrompt Caching

An agentic AI system governed by the MAGI — one soul refracted three ways. Three Claude models with deliberately designed flaws form an escalation ladder between you (the God) and the workers on the ground.

The flaw determines the rung: Haiku is blunt and cheap, consulted first. Sonnet is careful and formal, consulted second. Opus exhausts every thread and is consulted last — but only Casper (Haiku) renders the final say and may overrule them all. Workers never pray to the God directly. The ladder does.

Runs end-to-end on one machine. Append-only ledger. Source-available.

About

I'm an 18-year-old Computer Science & AI student at Mercer County Community College and an officer of the CSAI Club. I build things across a wide range — autonomous drones, neurotech experiments, video games, and AI-powered apps.

My work tends to live at the edges: where hardware meets software, where biology meets code, where games become something more. I'm driven by curiosity and the question of what's actually possible.

When I'm not coding, I'm building out my second brain in Obsidian, thinking about game design, or figuring out how to make a drone smarter.
