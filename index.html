About

I'm an 18-year-old Computer Science & AI student at Mercer County Community College and an officer of the CSAI Club. I build things across a wide range — autonomous drones, neurotech experiments, video games, and AI-powered apps.

My work tends to live at the edges: where hardware meets software, where biology meets code, where games become something more. I'm driven by curiosity and the question of what's actually possible.

When I'm not coding, I'm building out my second brain in Obsidian, thinking about game design, or figuring out how to make a drone smarter.
